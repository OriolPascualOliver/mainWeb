About

Opotek is the engineering team you call when you’re building a real product.
We design the full stack: Electronics, firmware, software, and mechanics. And make sure it all works together in the real world, not just on paper. You get tested, validated files ready for production.

What we do best

Design integrity. Manufacturing certainty.
We build complete embedded hardware systems: schematics, PCB design, enclosure CAD, prototyping, and system integration. You get production-ready deliverables: manufacturing files, test documentation, BOM support, and specs that work on the factory floor.

  • Concept to working prototype — Complete system design: electronics, firmware, and enclosure integrated from day one for your MVP.
  • Validation builds — EVT builds, bring-up, iteration, and validation.
  • Production support — Test fixtures, programming jigs, and DFM/DFT analysis for smooth scaling.
  • Embedded systems — MCU/RTOS firmware, drivers, and wireless connectivity (BLE, Wi-Fi, LoRa, cellular)..
  • Edge AI / ML — Sensor fusion, signal processing, and on-device inference for intelligent products.
  • Turnkey delivery — Coordinated mechanical, electrical, firmware, and software—one integrated product.

Outcome-focused deliverables: production files, test notes, BOM support, and documentation that survives manufacturing.
