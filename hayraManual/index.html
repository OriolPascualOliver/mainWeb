1Product overview

The Hayra ONE is an automatic feed dispenser designed for equestrian competitions and stable use. It lets you pre-load the exact amount of hay or feed your horse needs, set a precise dispensing time, and leave the rest to the device — giving you one less thing to worry about on competition day.

The device accepts a waterproof fold-seal bag, holds a measured portion of feed, and releases it at the scheduled time using a motorised buckle mechanism. Configuration is done via a built-in Wi-Fi access point from any smartphone or tablet.

2Loading the feed bag

Fill the bag, seal twice with magnetic strip, insert buckle until click, and hang securely from D-rings with the outlet downward.

3Power & battery

Recharge via USB-C. Battery lasts up to 7 days and can be checked via its 4 LEDs.

4Wi-Fi connection & scheduling

Connect to SSID HayraONE with password hayra2024. Captive portal should open automatically, or browse to 192.168.4.1.

5Configuration mode

Hold button while inserting battery for 2 seconds to access advanced settings (SSID, password, language).

6Button reference

Hold 5s to manually release the buckle and dispense immediately.

7LED status indicators
LED 1LED 2Status
Solid green OffStandby — schedule set, waiting
Blinking green OffWi-Fi access point active
Solid green Solid greenFeed dispensed successfully
8Cleaning & care

Remove battery first. Wipe body with damp cloth and rinse bag with clean water.

1Descripción del producto

El Hayra ONE es un dispensador automático de pienso diseñado para competiciones ecuestres y uso en establos.

2Carga de la bolsa de pienso

Llene, doble y selle la bolsa, inserte la hebilla y cuelgue el equipo de forma segura.

3Alimentación y batería

Batería recargable USB-C con autonomía de hasta 7 días.

4Conexión Wi-Fi y programación

Conéctese a HayraONE con contraseña hayra2024. Abra 192.168.4.1 si no aparece el portal.

5Modo de configuración

Mantener botón + insertar batería (2s) para cambiar SSID, clave e idioma.

6Referencia del botón

Mantener 5 segundos para apertura manual inmediata.
